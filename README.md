What is Pin-Chat-Analyze?
The methodology introduces a three-phase workflow:
# CHAT - Brainstorm with AI
Discuss your project goals, challenges, and ideas in natural conversation with an AI assistant.
# PIN - Mark What Matters  
Identify and pin key messages and activities that contain actionable information.
# ANALYZE - Let AI Structure  
The AI processes pinned content and automatically creates/updates your project's activity tree.
Result: Rapid transformation from unstructured ideas to organized, executable project plans.
